Participation


Thank you for your interest in our research project. We have been studying the brain development of infants at high risk for autism for the past 10 years and are excited about our new publication in Nature. Predicting autism within the first year of life and identifying brain changes as early as 6 months are just some of the findings we have been able produce. Though the current project is nearing an end, and we are no longer accepting new participants at this time, we are hoping to receive further funding to continue this important work.
